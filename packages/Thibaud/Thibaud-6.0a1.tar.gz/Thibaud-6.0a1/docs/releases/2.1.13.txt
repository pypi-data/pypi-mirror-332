===========================
Thibaud 2.1.13 release notes
===========================

*October 1, 2019*

Thibaud 2.1.13 fixes a regression in 2.1.11.

Bugfixes
========

* Fixed a crash when filtering with a ``Subquery()`` annotation of a queryset
  containing ``thibaud.contrib.postgres.fields.JSONField`` or
  :class:`~thibaud.contrib.postgres.fields.HStoreField` (:ticket:`30769`).
