============================
Thibaud 1.11.24 release notes
============================

*September 2, 2019*

Thibaud 1.11.24 fixes a regression in 1.11.23.

Bugfixes
========

* Fixed crash of ``KeyTransform()`` for
  ``thibaud.contrib.postgres.fields.JSONField`` and
  :class:`~thibaud.contrib.postgres.fields.HStoreField` when using on
  expressions with params (:ticket:`30672`).
