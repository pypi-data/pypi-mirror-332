===========================
Thibaud 2.0.13 release notes
===========================

*February 12, 2019*

Thibaud 2.0.13 fixes a regression in 2.0.12/2.0.11.

Bugfixes
========

* Fixed crash in ``thibaud.utils.numberformat.format_number()`` when the number
  has over 200 digits (:ticket:`30177`).
